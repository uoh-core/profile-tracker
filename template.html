about this site

I use this site primarily for making communication easier but also for some blogposting. I came to the conclusion of making this website primarily due to discord's policies and state of using AI and other algorithmic procedures to assist its moderation. Certain patterns & speech can suspend your account even if no ill intention is present, and I have yet to elaborate on that matter through blogposting which will come soon.

The main functionality behind this site is that I feed tokens locally and validate them via a discord endpoint, and then automate git push requests to update the static page with the response data which contains my account status.

Through the server widget below, you can join my discord server whose purpose is to stay in touch with friends but also banter & discuss topics such as otaku/bishoujo/moe culture, music related topics & some tech related stuff as well.
